SHNR

The Gentle Defender

The **SHNR** type is the quiet, nurturing soul. They are devoted to their human, preferring one or two close bonds over a crowd. They are deeply private but will emerge to ensure their family is safe and comfortable.

7 Key Traits of The Gentle Defender

  • 1. Supportive Quiet (S): Shies away from the spotlight and commotion. They recharge in quiet, hidden spaces and will retreat if the house gets too loud or busy.
  • 2. Fact-Based Caretaking (H): Focused on present, tangible needs—the warmth of the blanket, the taste of the water, the exact location of their favorite scratching post.
  • 3. Nurturing Compassion (N): Highly sensitive and concerned with the feelings of others. They are moved by affection and will respond immediately to soft, gentle calls.
  • 4. Regal Judgement (R): Finds comfort in fixed, established rituals. Their favorite routine is the predictability of being groomed or napping in your lap at the same time every night.
  • 5. The Silent Comfort: When you are unwell, they will quietly lie at the foot of your bed or on a nearby chair, offering a non-demanding, consistent presence.
  • 6. Overwhelmed by Intensity: Too much aggressive petting, rough play, or loud demands will cause them to shut down and seek a safe space until calm returns.
  • 7. Loyalty Above All: Their devotion is focused on a select few. Once they bond with a person, that bond is lifelong, and they will always put that person's emotional comfort first.
