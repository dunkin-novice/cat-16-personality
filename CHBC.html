Your Cat's Archetype Is:

CHBC

The Street CEO

The **CHBC** type is the practical, action-oriented cat. They are extroverted and focused on immediate results, always testing boundaries and seeking novelty. They are charmingly pushy and quick to adapt.

7 Key Traits of The Street CEO

  • 1. Extroverted Action (C): Lives in the moment and seeks immediate stimulation. If they are bored, they will make the excitement happen now, usually by knocking something off a shelf.
  • 2. Fact-Based Hunting (H): Concerned only with what works *right now*. They learn quickly by doing, focusing on the sensory details: the *feel* of the carpet, the *taste* of the air.
  • 3. Bossy Logic (B): Highly pragmatic and assertive. Their demands are clear, loud, and goal-oriented. They see affection as a transaction that leads to food or play.
  • 4. Chaos Perception (C): Hates being locked into a schedule. They are spontaneous, flexible, and value having all options open—especially the option to change their mind instantly.
  • 5. Boundary Tester: They view the house rules (like "no scratching the couch") as temporary suggestions to be tested and re-tested until enforcement stops.
  • 6. Hands-On Learner: When facing a problem (e.g., a closed drawer), they attack it directly with physical force, never wasting time on contemplation.
  • 7. The Opportunist: Always alert for new sensory input: a strange smell, a shadow, or a box that has just been opened. They must investigate immediately.
